About

A production company, house, studio, or team is a corporation that creates the physical foundation for works in the domains of performing arts, new media art, film, television, radio, comics, interactive arts, video games, websites, music, and video.Production teams are made up of technical personnel who work together to create media. In general, the word refers to anyone who is responsible for the technical components of generating a product, regardless of where their knowledge is needed or how long they have been working on it. In a theater production, for example, the production team includes the running staff as well as the theatrical producer, designers, and theatrical directing.
